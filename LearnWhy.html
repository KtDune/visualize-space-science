Why Space Science

Why are we examining
in space?

Understand

To understand the fundamental ways life uses gravity to regulate and sustain its growth, metabolism, reproduction and development, and also how it repairs damage and protects itself from infection and disease

Discover

Discover how organisms respond to space, develop physiological models, identify underlying mechanisms, promote open science, and create technologies for space research and human health support

Observe

Effects observed in astronauts are similar to accelerated aging and health problems caused by a sedentary lifestyle; studying the human body in space for six months gives us data that would take years to gather on Earth.

Improve

Improve the quality of life on Earth such as reducing the impacts of reduced levels of physical activity and issues that affect older adult populations on Earth
